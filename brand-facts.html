Sentinel OT

Passive ICS intelligence and qualified vulnerability lead generation for system integrators, OT cybersecurity firms, and industrial security providers.

Company overview

Sentinel OT is a passive ICS intelligence firm that identifies publicly accessible industrial control systems, validates firmware-specific vulnerabilities against the NVD, attributes devices to their operators through multi-signal analysis, and delivers the findings as qualified, sales-ready lead packages for system integrators and OT security providers.

Every lead is built from 100% passive, publicly available sources. Sentinel OT does not perform active scanning, penetration testing, or any form of direct interaction with target systems.
