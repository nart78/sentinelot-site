Privacy Policy

Effective: February 22, 2026

Sentinel OT ("we", "us", "our") operates sentinelot.com. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Contact information: When you email us or request a sample lead, we receive your name and email address. We do not use contact forms or collect information automatically through our website.

No cookies or tracking: This website does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or third-party tracking. We do not collect browsing behavior, device fingerprints, or IP addresses of website visitors.

2. Intelligence Data

Our services involve the collection and analysis of publicly available information relating to industrial, commercial, and critical infrastructure. All intelligence is derived entirely from passive, publicly available sources. We do not perform active scanning, penetration testing, or any form of intrusive probing.

3. How We Use Information

Contact data: Solely to respond to your inquiries, deliver requested sample leads, and communicate about our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact information with third parties.

Intelligence data: Packaged and delivered to authorized clients for lawful, defensive security purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We share intelligence reports only with authorized clients who have agreed to use the data for lawful, defensive purposes.

We do not sell bulk data, publish operator identities publicly, or share intelligence with unauthorized parties.

5. Data Retention

Contact information is retained only as long as necessary to maintain the business relationship. Intelligence data is retained in our internal systems and updated as new information becomes available.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting us.

If you are an operator whose facility information appears in our reports and wish to discuss our findings, we welcome the conversation. Our goal is to support modernization, not to cause harm.

7. Canadian Privacy Law

Sentinel OT is based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information in our possession, including encrypted communications and access controls on internal systems.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to us via our contact form.
